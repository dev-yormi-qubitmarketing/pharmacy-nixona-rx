FAQ

Got Questions? We’ve Got Answers!

Don’t be a stranger, contact us! Our team is standing by and can by reached by phone or fax to answer your questions and get your medications filled. During business hours don’t hesitate to stop in our store and talk to our staff one-on-one.

Customer Service:

1
How do I make an appointment?
You can fill out an online form, call and we also accept walk-ins.
2
Do you only sell medications?
No. We are a diverse store and even offer skincare solutions.
3
Do you offer counseling?
We offer multiple counseling services from free prescription counseling to medical management therapy, which is more comprehensive. Call or fill out an online form to make a counseling appointment.

Other Questions

4
Do you accept insurance?
Yes. We will work with you and your healthcare provider to get you coverage.
5
Can I pay for my prescription with a check?
Yes. We accept all forms of payment.
6
Do you deliver medications?
Yes. We delivery to both home and work locations.
7
How long do medication deliveries take to arrive?
You will receive your medication within 3-5 business days, provided all insurance and doctor’s information goes through.
